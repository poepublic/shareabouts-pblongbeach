{{#_}}Participatory Budgeting Long Beach{{/_}}

{{#_}}is an innovative democratic process that gives Long Beach residents of the 9th and 1st districts the power to decide how to spend city funds in their district.{{/_}}

{{#_}}Idea submission for the PB process the 1st and 9th Districts is now open! If you have an idea for a project to improve your district, please share it on the map!{{/_}} {{#_}}In the Spring, community members will review all the ideas we have received, and develop them into full proposals. In May, all district residents will vote on the top projects.{{/_}}
